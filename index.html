National Flag

Description Colors:

Dark green and white.

Layout:

Green Field:

Represents the Muslim majority and prosperity.

White Stripe:

Symbolizes non-Muslim minorities and peace.

Crescent and Star:

White crescent stands for progress; white star for light and knowledge.

History:

Adoption:

Officially adopted on August 14, 1947.

Design:

Based on the flag of the All-India Muslim League.

Symbolism:

Green reflects Islamic heritage, white signifies peaceful coexistence, and the crescent and star are traditional Islamic symbols. This flag embodies Pakistan’s identity, values, and heritage.

Historical Events

  • The Creation of the Flag Design: The flag design was created by Ameeruddin Kidwai, with input from the All-India Muslim League, reflecting Islamic symbolism and aspirations.
  • Adoption of the Flag: The flag was officially adopted on August 14, 1947, marking the day Pakistan became an independent nation.
  • First Use of the Flag: The flag was first hoisted in front of the Karachi Governor’s House during the independence ceremony.
